General Questions

What is Expense Tracker?

Expense Tracker is a platform designed to help users manage their finances by tracking incomes and expenses.

How do I sign up for Budget Buddy?

Signing up for Budget Buddy is easy! Simply visit our website and follow the prompts to create your account.

Is Budget Buddy accessible from any location?

Yes, Budget Buddy is accessible from anywhere with an internet connection. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, you can easily manage your finances using Budget Buddy.

Is Budget Buddy available for desktop computers?

Yes, Budget Buddy is accessible via web browsers on desktop computers, allowing you to manage your finances conveniently from your PC or laptop.

How do I get support?

If you need assistance, you can contact our support team via email.

Account and Features

Is my financial data safe with Budget Buddy?

Yes, we take the security and privacy of your financial data very seriously. Budget Buddy uses encryption and follows industry best practices to keep your information safe.

Can I categorize my expenses in Budget Buddy?

Absolutely! Budget Buddy lets you categorize expenses into predefined categories to fit your needs.

How do I track my income in Budget Buddy?

To track your income, go to the "Income" section, click "Add Income," enter the amount, select the source (like salary or freelance work), and choose the date.

Is there a way to visualize my spending trends in Budget Buddy?

Absolutely! Budget Buddy offers various visualization tools, such as graphs and charts, to help you visualize your spending trends over time. This allows you to identify patterns, spot areas for improvement, and make informed decisions about your finances.

How do I delete a transaction in Budget Buddy?

To delete a transaction in Budget Buddy, simply navigate to the transaction list, find the transaction you want to remove, and select the delete option. Confirm the deletion, and the transaction will be removed from your records.
